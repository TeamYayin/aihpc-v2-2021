International Conference on AI and High Performance Computing (AIHPC 2022).

Online on September 10th & 11th, 2022.

The conference is in submission for the affiliation of Elsevier. We cordially invite you to submit your research papers on the lisited conference tracks.

Authors are invited to submit complete unpublished papers, which are not under review in any other conference or journal, to https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=aihpc2022.

Selected papers will be further considered for possible publication in a Special Issue of the Computers journal (pending).

Position papers are also welcome and should be clearly marked as such.
